Cuisine

Cuisine



Babru


This is a famous dish of Manali. It belongs to the family of kachori (a crisp snack of India). This is the most famous black gram stuffed puri with a delicious taste. Babru is prepared from traditional methods which are deep-fried and served with chutney.


This dish perfectly tastes with tamarind chutney. It is a successful dish that brings all the flavours and aroma of Manali and Himachal Pradesh’s cuisine.




Aktori


Aktori is a type of cake which is prepared at the festivals of Manali. It is made from buckwheat. You should try this unique dish when you visit Manali. It is prepared from the traditional methods of Himachal Pradesh.


The texture of this delicious dish is like pancakes. You can enjoy this dish with the topping of honey and relish this Pahari dish’s sweet flavours.




Chana Madra


Chana Madra is a famous Pahari dish of Himachal Pradesh. It is a yoghurt based curry made from chickpeas and rice. The process of making this dish is traditional. Chana Madra is prepared by cooking chickpeas in yoghurt gravy slowly with rice mixture.


This dish is rich in vitamins, fibres, proteins and minerals. The taste of this pahari dish is heavenly and a bucket of goodness. This pahari dish can be tasted best with hot steaming rice.




Tudkiya Bhath


Tudkiya Bhath is the most famous and delicious dish of Manali. It is a great recipe made with rice, lentils and grounded species. It is prepared by simmering the rice with soaked masoor dal. Rare spices prepare this traditional dish with a classic Pahari touch.


Tudkiya Bhath can be enjoyed alone with a burst of flavours in each bite. You can enjoy this delicious dish of Manali with your family members and friends.




Bhey


Bhey is one of the most famous dishes in Manali. It is a popular dish in Himachal Pradesh prepared with lotus stems. This unique dish is prepared by boiling and stir-frying the thin-sliced washed lotus with some spices and gram flour.


The texture of this dish is golden brown and crispy. It is also known as spicy lotus stem. This can be enjoyed along with all your family members and friends too.




Sidu


Sidu is one of the most loved local dishes of Manali. It is prepared by kneading wheat flour with yeast and resting it for rising well. It is stuffed with mutton and can be enjoyed alone. The stuffing is loaded into the dough and then steamed. This cuisine is loved by most non-vegetarians.
